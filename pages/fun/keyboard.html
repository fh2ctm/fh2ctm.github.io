Keyboards that I use or have used.

Prebuilt

Keyboards that you use out of the box instead of customizing.

Logitech MX Mini

Nice little board. Battery life is great and haven't had any connectivity issues with it. Unfortunately one of the keys died after a drop, and didn't find a way to disassemble to fix it.

HHKB Hybrid Type-S

A simple Topre board. Many people's grail.

I owned one of these in snow white with blank keycaps. It looked amazing. However, I did not like the typing experience as some have. That and I wasn't able to customize the key layout to my liking.

Sold for a good price. I really wish I had liked it more.

Logitech K780

I really like the design of this keyboard. As for typing experience, nothing amazing.

Custom

Keyboards that you customize.

Keychron Q3

My first custom keyboard. Build quality's solid, but the paint job is kinda bad. Did some tape mod, added foam, dampeners, and spray painted black.

Sold eventually due to the height feeling wrong.

Drop CSTM65

Current use. Solid build, IMO the nicest shine-through keycaps you can get.

Listening to Music is a Pain

I was happy with using my walkman, but I started to get ear infections from wearing the IEMs for too long :( I find myself missing the over-ears and the DAC on my desk, but unfortunately I cannot carry them with me.

Managing library is another headache... ALAC is a lot better FLAC with better metadata support and it's the only modern lossless format that the Apple Music app on my MacBook supports. I'd use it everywhere, except all the sources I can get are FLACs, which means I have to download them, convert them to ALACs, load them onto my Walkman, and load them into my Apple Music library on my Mac.

If it were an iPod, I'd still be able to just sync it, which would've been so much better! Sadly we will never have a new iPod again :(
